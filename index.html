About Us

CSI JUET is the Student Branch of the Computer Society of India at Jaypee University of Engineering & Technology, Guna and our Student Branch has been functioning here at JUET from 2006 onwards. Various Technical Events are organized in every Semester.

Services

What We Do.

CSI JUET comprises of 4 different clubs specializing in their respective fields. Students are selected and placed in one of these clubs based on their expertise in that particular field.

Programming Club

The Prgramming Club aims to promote and develop the programming culture by conducting various events, workshops, code classes and kodeathons (programming contests) that enhance the knowledge and experience of programming.

Development Club

The Development Club provides you with the opportunity to take part in all the workshops offered by Development Club and learn various skills. Whether you are interested in web, mobile or game development, this is an opportunity for you to learn comprehensively and constructively.

Multimedia Club

Multimedia Club introduces students to multimedia and animation software that allow them to combine photos, narration, animation and videos to create movies, artwork and more! Multimedia club gives students a chance to explore their creativity and challenge their potential.

Publication Club

Publication Club is a group of enthusiastic writers, editors, authors, communications professional, public relations or media specialist who love what they do. Publication Club aims to deliver all the news ASAP while also managing the ongoing and forthcoming events of CSI.
